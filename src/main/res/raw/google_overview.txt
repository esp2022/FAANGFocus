Google Overview


Google LLC is a multinational technology company specializing in internet-related services and products. It is widely recognized for its search engine, online advertising technologies, cloud computing, software, and hardware.

Number of Employees
--------------------------------------
As of 2023, Google employs over 190,000 people worldwide.

Revenue
--------------------------------------
In 2023, Google's parent company Alphabet reported annual revenue of approximately $282 billion, driven largely by advertising and cloud services.

Headquarters
--------------------------------------
Google's headquarters, known as the Googleplex, is located in Mountain View, California. The company has numerous offices worldwide, supporting its global operations.

Company Culture
--------------------------------------
Google is renowned for its open and innovative company culture. It emphasizes creativity and collaboration, offering employees a wide range of benefits and perks. The work environment is designed to foster productivity and innovation, with facilities such as on-site gyms, free meals, and flexible work arrangements.
