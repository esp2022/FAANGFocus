Amazon Overview


Amazon.com, Inc. is a multinational technology company based in Seattle, Washington. It is recognized as the world's largest online retailer and a significant player in the fields of cloud computing, digital streaming, and artificial intelligence.

Number of Employees
--------------------------------------
As of 2023, Amazon employs approximately 1.54 million full- and part-time workers globally, making it the second-largest private employer in the United States.

Revenue
--------------------------------------
In 2023, Amazon reported annual revenue of approximately $514 billion, driven by its diverse business segments including e-commerce, cloud computing (AWS), and advertising services.

Headquarters
--------------------------------------
Amazon's headquarters are located in Seattle, Washington. The company also has a significant presence in Arlington, Virginia, where it is developing its second headquarters, known as HQ2.

Company Culture
--------------------------------------
Amazon's culture is known for its focus on innovation and customer obsession. The company operates on principles that emphasize long-term thinking, operational excellence, and a willingness to take bold risks. While Amazon encourages a high-performance environment, it has faced criticism for its demanding work culture.
