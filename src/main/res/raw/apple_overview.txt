Apple Overview


Apple Inc. is a multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California. It is renowned for its innovative products and services, including the iPhone, iPad, Mac computers, Apple Watch, and Apple TV.


Number of Employees
--------------------------------------
As of 2023, Apple has approximately 164,000 employees worldwide.


Revenue
--------------
In 2023, Apple's revenue was approximately $394 billion, primarily driven by sales of its consumer electronics and services.

Headquarters
------------------------
The company's headquarters is located at Apple Park in Cupertino, a state-of-the-art campus known for its circular design and sustainable architecture.

Company Culture
------------------------------
Apple is known for its strong emphasis on innovation and design excellence. The corporate culture encourages creativity and collaboration among employees while maintaining a high standard of quality in its products. Employees often describe the work environment as fast-paced and challenging but rewarding.

Apple continues to lead in the tech industry by focusing on user experience and integrating cutting-edge technology into its products.
