Netflix Overview


Netflix, Inc. is a leading entertainment services provider, offering streaming services for TV series, films, and games across over 190 countries. Founded in 1997, Netflix has grown to become a major player in the media industry with a focus on original content and global reach.

Number of Employees
--------------------------------------
As of 2023, Netflix employs approximately 13,000 people worldwide.

Revenue
--------------------------------------
In 2023, Netflix reported an annual revenue of approximately $36.30 billion, driven by its diverse range of streaming content and subscription services.

Headquarters
--------------------------------------
Netflix is headquartered in Los Gatos, California, USA. The company also operates international offices in various countries including Canada, France, Brazil, the Netherlands, India, and Japan.

Company Culture
--------------------------------------
Netflix is known for its unique corporate culture that emphasizes "people over process." The company values high performance and encourages employees to take ownership of their work. Netflix fosters an environment where innovation and creativity are highly valued, aiming to empower employees to make impactful decisions.
